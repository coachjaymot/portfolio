Tennis Overhead

Quick reactions and compact swings are essential for effective net play in tennis.

Key Volley Fundamentals

Mastering the volley requires early preparation, balanced footwork, and controlled contact.

Grip

  • Use a continental grip.

    • Imagine You Are Serving

  • I firmly believe in transferable skills. The overhead can be very similar to one's serving motion with one exception. A serve includes a toss that is in the server's control. An overhead is determined by your opponent. Some are easier than others to get to, but preparing by moving to a space that gives you a serve-like comfort is always good. Racket belongs overhead and slighlty in front of you upon contact. Use the arm that you would use for the serve toss as a pointer/guide hand to track the ball.

    • Side To Target with Both Hands on The Racket

  • Turn to the side with both hands on the racket. Having both hands on the racket during the turn motion encourages one to position their feet properly. Too many people remain in an open stance and end up falling because they end up back pedaling on their heels in this open stance. Pickleball players are more inclined to stay open stance at first due to the presence of the kitchen. A lot of falls in pickleball because people do not get in the habit of turning side to target.
