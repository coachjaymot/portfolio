Helpful Tips

Essential concepts for defending, attacking, and transitioning effectively in full court 1 on 1.

Defensive Stance

  • Stay low and balanced to react quickly to the ball handler.

    • Anticipation

  • Read your opponent’s hips and anticipate their direction.

    • Controlled Closeout

  • Contest without fouling—short, choppy steps into a stable stance.

    • Contain the Dribbler

  • Angle them to the sideline and reduce open space.

    • Change of Pace

  • Explode after slowing down to create separation.

    • Attack Angles

  • Attack outside the defender’s frame to force recovery.

    • Crossover Moves

  • Use deceleration, hesitation, and fake footwork to score.

    • Scan Ahead

  • Read open space early to dictate the action.

    • Recover Quickly

  • Immediate sprint after misses or turnovers to stay competitive.

    • Push the Pace

  • Apply pressure early before the defender resets.

    • Spacing

  • Use the width of the court to stretch the defender.

    • Read Tendencies

  • Notice whether they overplay speed, angle, or physicality.
