Creating a System for Play Calls

Organize your offensive and defensive strategies with a structured play call system for consistent team execution.

Play Call Categorization

Organizing play calls doesnâ€™t have to be complex. In reality, the simpler your play calling system is, the better. For my teams I kept this really simple. Defenses were represented by colors. Offenses were represented by locations (cities, states). Presses and Pressbreaks were numbers. Sideline out of bounds (SLOBs) and baseline out of bounds were shapes.

Defenses = Colors

  • Green = Man to Man Help Defense
  • Red = Man to Man Denial Defense
  • Blue = 2-3 Zone
  • White = 1-2-2 Zone
  • Black = 3-2 Zone
  • Purple = 1-3-1 Zone

    • Offenses = Locations (Cities/States)

  • Indiana = 1-3-1 Zone Offense
  • Pittsburg = 1-3-1 Zone Offense with pass/cut away action and low post-high post flashes
  • Vegas = 2-1-2 Zone Offense
  • Florida = Flex

    • Presses = Numbers

  • 40 = 2-2-1 Press
  • 12 = 1-2-1-1 Press
